Dudo Dice lets you play Perudo online against AI or friends.

Dudo Dice is a free browser version of the classic liar's dice game, also known as Perudo. You can start a quick solo match against computer opponents, open an online room for friends, or review the full rules before your first round. The game includes support for classic bidding, wild ones, Palifico rounds, optional Calza calls, guest profiles, and real-time multiplayer sessions that work across desktop and mobile devices.

Play Dudo online your way

If you want to practise strategy, head into single-player mode and adjust the number of opponents, starting dice, and challenge settings. If you are ready for live matches, the online lobby lets you create private rooms, join shared room codes, or jump into public tables. Players who are new to Perudo can also read a full rules guide and FAQ that explains bidding, challenges, Palifico, Calza, and how to win a round.
